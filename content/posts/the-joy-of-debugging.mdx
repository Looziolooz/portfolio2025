Debugging is detective work. It's where logic meets intuition.

## How to Enjoy Debugging

- **Slow down**: Rushing leads to missed clues.
- **Ask better questions**: What changed? What assumptions are false?
- **Celebrate breakthroughs**: Every fix is a triumph.

## Conclusion

Debugging is where growth happens. Embrace the process, and you'll love the results.
