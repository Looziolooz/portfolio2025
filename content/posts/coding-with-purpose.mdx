Purpose gives your work direction. It turns effort into meaning.

## How to Find Purpose

- **Reflect on values**: What matters most to you?
- **Choose meaningful projects**: Align with causes you care about.
- **Measure impact**: Track how your work helps others.

## Conclusion

When you code with purpose, every line matters. Build what you believe in.
