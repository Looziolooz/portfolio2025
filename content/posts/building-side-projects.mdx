Side projects are playgrounds for your imagination.

## Why Side Projects Matter

- **Freedom to explore**: No deadlines, no constraints.
- **Skill development**: Try new tools and techniques.
- **Portfolio building**: Show what you love to build.

## Conclusion

Your next big idea might start as a side project. Make time to tinker.
