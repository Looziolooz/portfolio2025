Mentorship is a two-way street. It’s about connection, guidance, and mutual growth.

## How to Be a Great Mentor

- **Listen deeply**: Understand before advising.
- **Share stories**: Experience is more powerful than theory.
- **Encourage autonomy**: Guide, don’t dictate.

## Conclusion

Mentorship builds bridges. Invest in others, and you’ll grow too.
