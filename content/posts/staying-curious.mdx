Curiosity is the spark behind every breakthrough.

## How to Stay Curious

- **Ask why**: Dig deeper into how things work.
- **Explore new tech**: Try unfamiliar languages or paradigms.
- **Read widely**: Learn from other disciplines.

## Conclusion

Curiosity keeps you evolving. Never stop asking questions.
